How Questions Are Selected

Basic idea

The quiz selects questions based on the filters you choose before starting. You can filter by JLPT level, difficulty, mastery level, and number of questions.

JLPT level filter

You can choose all levels, N1 only, or N2 only. If you choose N1 only, only N1 grammar questions will appear.

Difficulty filter

You can choose all difficulties, easy, normal, or hard. This controls which question difficulty will appear.

Mastery level filter

Each grammar point has a mastery level: new, learning, weak, or mastered. You can choose one or more mastery levels before starting the quiz.

Mastery Meaning
New Grammar points that have not been studied much yet.
Learning Grammar points you have started practicing.
Weak Grammar points that need review because of repeated mistakes.
Mastered Grammar points with strong accuracy after repeated correct answers.

Weighted selection

When several matching questions are available, the app gives priority to grammar points that need more review.

In general, weak and learning grammar points are more likely to appear than mastered grammar points.

Cooldown system

The app temporarily avoids recently shown questions so the same question does not repeat immediately.

If the filter is too narrow and no question is found, the cooldown can be cleared so the quiz can continue.

Unlimited mode

If you choose unlimited mode, the quiz continues until you press the finish button. Your current session stats will be shown while you continue answering.
